## ðŸŽ¯ KEY METRICS TO TRACK

### North Star Metric
**Monthly Active Concert-Goers**: Users who view/attend/review at least 1 event per month

### User Metrics
- **DAU/MAU**: Daily/Monthly active users
- **Retention**: 1-day, 7-day, 30-day, 90-day
- **Engagement**: Reviews written, events viewed, interactions per session
- **Conversion**: Free â†’ Premium rate
- **Churn**: Monthly subscription cancellations
- **NPS**: Net Promoter Score

### Revenue Metrics
- **MRR/ARR**: Monthly/Annual Recurring Revenue
- **ARPU**: Average Revenue Per User
- **LTV**: Customer Lifetime Value
- **CAC**: Customer Acquisition Cost
- **LTV:CAC**: Lifetime value to acquisition cost ratio (target 3:1 minimum)
- **Payback Period**: Months to recover CAC (target <12 months)

### Marketplace Metrics
- **Supply Coverage**: % of concerts/venues with profiles
- **Review Density**: Avg reviews per event
- **Creator Activation**: % of artists claiming profiles
- **Venue Activation**: % of venues claiming profiles
